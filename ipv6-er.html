EdgeRouter IPv6 & Spectrum (Charter)

Spectrum (formerly Charter) has solid IPv6 networking and support, granting each customer a /56 and in my region, allowing up to 3 prefixes. While the company lags horribly on upload speed and has never been considered providing great customer value nor support, its technical plant for IP has been quite good.

There were some initial problems, the fixes for which are noted at the bottom of this document.

These are the configuration details for IPv6 on an Edgerouter.

Zone Based Firewall

Before beginning, zone-policy needs to be configured to provide firewalls for both IPv4 and IPv6. Minimal zones will be for a local (the router itself), an internal lan and wan. Any additional networks filtered by the router, such as a dmz or work vlan will need to be zones, but these additions are not part of this configuration.
